Welcome to AP Mastery

The ultimate mastery-based learning platform for AP Computer Science Principles.

Our Philosophy

AP Mastery focuses on granular mastery. Instead of one big test, students master individual topics through bite-sized, data-driven quizzes. They can't move forward until they prove they've mastered the basics.

1. Create Your Class

Start by creating a class. You'll get a unique 6-character Join Code to share with your students.

  • Set a Retake Wait Time to encourage studying between attempts.
  • Choose your Progression Mode (Linear, Unlocked, or Manual).
🏫
📊

2. The Live Matrix

Click on any class to see the Progress Matrix. It's a real-time grid showing every student's status across the entire curriculum.

  • Colors tell the story: Green is mastery, Yellow is in-progress, Red needs help.
  • Click a student's name for a deep-dive report.

3. Item Analysis

Our powerful analytics show you the Top 10 Most Missed Questions for every unit and topic.

  • See exactly which answer choices are confusing your students.
  • Identify common misconceptions across your whole class in seconds.
🧠
📝

4. Practice Exams

Ready for the big day? Use the Practice Test feature to assign a 50-question mock exam.

  • Questions are randomly drawn from every topic.
  • Proportions strictly follow the official AP CSP weighting.
🚀
